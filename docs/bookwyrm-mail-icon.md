# Bookwyrm Mail Icon

The Bookwyrm Mail icon should feel related to the Bookwyrm Reader icon without
copying it directly.

Design intent:

- Shared family language: dark fantasy wyrm, antique gold trim, parchment, warm
  dramatic lighting, premium app-icon rendering.
- Product distinction: mail is represented by a sealed parchment envelope rather
  than an open book or reader title banner.
- Avoid readable text, title banners, copied pose/composition, generic blue mail
  metaphors, or anything too cartoonish.

Generated assets:

- `src/mailwyrm/static/icons/bookwyrm-mail-icon.png` is the current preferred
  1024px app-icon source.
- `src/mailwyrm/static/icons/bookwyrm-mail-icon-candidate-1.png` is a circular
  envelope-forward concept.
- `src/mailwyrm/static/icons/bookwyrm-mail-icon-candidate-2.png` is the selected
  manuscript/envelope source concept at generation size.
- `src/mailwyrm/static/icons/bookwyrm-mail-icon-candidate-3.png` is a mailbox
  and message-chest concept.
